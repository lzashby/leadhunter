Pricing questions

Do I need a credit card to start? +

No. The free plan requires no payment details at all. You only need a card when you upgrade to Pro or Agency.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes — cancel anytime from your dashboard. No contracts, no penalties. You keep access until the end of your billing period.

What happens when I hit my search limit? +

Free users are notified when they reach 5 searches and can upgrade to continue. Pro and Agency users have unlimited searches with no interruptions.

Is there a free trial for Pro? +

Yes — you get a 7-day free trial on Pro. If you're not satisfied, cancel within 7 days for a full refund.

Do you offer discounts for annual plans? +

Yes — paying annually saves you 30%. Pro drops from $49/mo to $34/mo, and Agency from $149/mo to $104/mo.
