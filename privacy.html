Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

LeadHunter ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — name, email address, and password when you register
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe; we do not store your card details
  • Usage data — searches performed, features used, and pages visited
  • Device data — browser type, IP address, and operating system for security and analytics purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the LeadHunter service
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send service updates, security notices, and support responses
  • To analyse usage trends and improve the product
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Business Data We Surface

LeadHunter surfaces publicly available business information (names, phone numbers, addresses, websites) from sources including Google Maps and public business directories. This is business contact data — not personal consumer data. We do not collect, store, or sell private individual data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • Analytics providers — to understand how the product is used (anonymised)
  • Legal authorities — if required by law

5. Cookies

We use cookies to keep you logged in and to understand how users interact with the platform. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

6. GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also object to or restrict processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@leadhunterai.com.

Our lawful basis for processing your data is contractual necessity (to provide the service you signed up for) and legitimate interest (for product improvement and security).

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect data in transit. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe, which is PCI DSS compliant.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on the platform. Continued use of LeadHunter after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@leadhunterai.com or visit our contact page.
