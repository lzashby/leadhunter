About LeadHunter

Built for people who
hate wasting time

LeadHunter was built with one mission — help agencies and freelancers find the right businesses to work with, faster than ever before.

Why we built this

Every agency owner knows the pain. You spend hours manually searching Google, copying business names into a spreadsheet, trying to find phone numbers and emails — and half the data is wrong by the time you use it.

We built LeadHunter to eliminate that entirely. Type a niche and a city. Get 100 qualified leads in under 10 seconds. Complete with phone numbers, emails, owner names, website status, review counts, and an AI score telling you exactly which businesses are worth contacting first.

We're focused on one thing — making lead generation so fast and easy that anyone can do it, whether you're a solo freelancer or running a team of 10.
