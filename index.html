FAQ
Common questions
Where does the data come from? +

LeadHunter pulls data from Google Maps, Google Business Profiles, and public business directories. All data is publicly available and sourced ethically. We do not scrape private data or bypass any access controls.

Is the data GDPR compliant? +

Yes. All data LeadHunter provides is publicly available business information — not personal consumer data. Business contact details published publicly are classified as legitimate interest data under GDPR. We recommend always including an unsubscribe option in your outreach.

How accurate is the data? +

Phone numbers and addresses are sourced directly from Google Business Profiles, which business owners maintain themselves. Email accuracy varies — we use pattern matching and verification where possible. Data is refreshed regularly and typically verified within the last 30 days.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes, absolutely. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard and you won't be charged again. You keep access until the end of your billing period.

Does it work for any country? +

Yes — LeadHunter covers 190+ countries worldwide. Data quality is highest in English-speaking countries (US, UK, Australia, Canada, NZ) and major European and Asian cities. Coverage improves continuously as our data sources expand.
